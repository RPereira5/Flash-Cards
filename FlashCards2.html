Help

New to Flash Cards Tool?

  • Create your first card. Add a title and description.
  • Click on the new card to reveal its description, as well as various buttons:
    • The "Details" button shows you your card's title, description, date and time created, and when it was last modified.
    • The "Edit" button allows you to change your card's title, description, or both.
    • The "Delete" button removes the card from your account.
  • Sort your cards by title, date created, and date modified.
  • If you prefer, change the page theme to light with the "Switch to Light Theme" button.
  • Log in or sign up to save your cards.
